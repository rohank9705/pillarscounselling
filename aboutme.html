About Me

Creating a safe, non-judgmental space for your healing journey.

Hello, and welcome. I am a compassionate, client-focused Cognitive Behavioural Therapist dedicated to supporting individuals through anxiety, depression, stress, and low self-esteem. My practice is built on providing a safe, collaborative, and deeply inclusive environment. I have extensive experience working with a wonderfully diverse range of clients—including neurodivergent individuals, veterans, and professionals from various cultural backgrounds. Specialising in structured, evidence-based CBT, we will work together to set clear, achievable goals tailored to your unique needs. Whether we are utilising practical tools like cognitive restructuring and behavioural activation, or developing safe coping strategies for moments of crisis, my aim is to equip you with the skills you need for lasting emotional resilience. Backed by rigorous training in safeguarding, I am here to provide a secure space where you can safely explore your challenges and achieve meaningful, positive change.

Training & Core Competencies

  • Specialized Support for Anxiety, Depression, Stress & Self-Esteem
  • Evidence-Based CBT Techniques (Cognitive Restructuring, Behavioural Activation & Exposure)
  • Inclusive Therapy for Diverse & Neurodivergent Populations
  • ASIST Suicide Prevention & Crisis Safety Planning
  • Rigorous Safeguarding, Confidentiality & Ethical Practice
